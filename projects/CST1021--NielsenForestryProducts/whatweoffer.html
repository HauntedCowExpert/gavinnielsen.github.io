What We Offer

Custom Cut Slab Wood


Slab wood can make beautiful tables, benches, and many other things.

Custom Wooden Cookies

Wooden Cookeis are great for placeholders on tables for special ocassions or cutting boards. Cookies can be cut to custom thickness and widths, and with or without bark.

Custom Cut Lumber

Custom cut lumber can be any length, width, thickness, or wood type and is excelent for great looking projects from large to small.

Firewood

Our firewood can be cut to custom lengths and sizes, with custom size bundles available. Our firewood is made from oak wood, with other woods available upon requeast and availability.
