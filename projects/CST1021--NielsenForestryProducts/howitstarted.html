How It Started

Nielsen Forestry Products started as a hobby business only being run during the evenings and weekends outside of normal work hours. During the summer of 2018 Nielsen Forestry Products became a full time company and job. It has since had one full time employee and several family helpers. It continues to be a single owner/operator company with help from family members.
